## Provincial and Territorial Tax Credits and Rates

### British Columbia Tax Credits

#### Interactive Digital Media Tax Credit
- **Eligibility**: Interactive digital media corporations registered as eligible business corporations in the small business venture capital program
- **Thresholds**: $100,000 and $2,000,000 (prorated for short tax years)
- **Filing Requirements**: 
  - File Schedule 429, British Columbia Interactive Digital Media Tax Credit
  - Claim no later than 18 months after tax year end
  - Enter credit amount on line 680 of Schedule 5
- **Reference**: Part 10, British Columbia Income Tax Act

#### Shipbuilding and Ship Repair Industry Tax Credit
- **Eligibility**: Eligible employers in British Columbia shipbuilding and ship repair industry
- **Timeframe**: Extended to December 31, 2026 (previously December 31, 2024)
- **Employee Requirements**: Must be registered in prescribed program through SkilledTradesBC before January 1, 2025
- **Credit Types**:
  - Basic tax credit: 20% of salary and wages, maximum $5,250 per employee
  - Completion tax credit: 20% of salary and wages, maximum $5,250 per employee
  - Enhanced tax credit: 30% of salary and wages, maximum $7,875 per employee (for individuals registered under Indian Act or qualifying for disability amount)
- **Filing Requirements**:
  - File Schedule 430, British Columbia Shipbuilding and Ship Repair Industry Tax Credit
  - Claim no later than 36 months after tax year end
  - Enter total credit amount on line 681 of Schedule 5
- **Reference**: Part 9, British Columbia Income Tax Act

#### Clean Buildings Tax Credit
- **Eligibility**: Retrofits improving energy efficiency of multi-unit residential buildings (4+ units) and prescribed commercial buildings
- **Timeframe**: 
  - Expenditures made before April 1, 2025
  - Agreements entered after February 22, 2022
  - Retrofits completed before April 1, 2026
  - Certification application deadline: September 30, 2027
- **Credit Rate**: 5% of qualifying expenditures (refundable)
- **Filing Requirements**:
  - Enter certificate number on line 884 of Schedule 5 (for single certificate)
  - File Schedule 432 for multiple certificates
  - Enter credit amount on line 685 of Schedule 5
- **Reference**: Part 16, British Columbia Income Tax Act

### Yukon Tax Rates and Credits

#### Tax Rates
- **Lower Rate**: 0% (effective January 1, 2021, previously 2%)
- **Higher Rate**: 12%
- **Business Limit**: $500,000
- **Calculation**: Use Schedule 443, Yukon Corporation Tax Calculation (not filed with return)
- **Reporting**: Enter tax amount on line 245 of Schedule 5

#### Political Contribution Tax Credit
- **Eligibility**: Contributions to registered political party or candidate for Yukon Legislative Assembly
- **Maximum Credit**: $650 annually
- **Calculation**:
  - 75% of first $400 contributed
  - Plus 50% of next $350 contributed
  - Plus 33 1/3% of next $525 contributed
- **Reporting**: 
  - Enter contributions on line 897 of Schedule 5
  - Enter credit on line 675 of Schedule 5

#### Manufacturing and Processing Profits Tax Credit
- **Credit Rate**: 9.5% (small business increment: 0% effective January 1, 2021)
- **Calculation**: Use Schedule 440, Yukon Manufacturing and Processing Profits Tax Credit (not filed with return)
- **Reporting**: Enter credit amount on line 677 of Schedule 5

#### Research and Development Tax Credit
- **Eligibility**: Corporations with permanent establishment in Yukon incurring qualified expenditures for SR&ED in Yukon
- **Credit Components**:
  - 15% of eligible expenditures
  - 5% of eligible expenditures paid to Yukon University
- **Nature**: Fully refundable (first applied against total taxes payable)
- **Filing Requirements**:
  - File Schedule 442, Yukon Research and Development Tax Credit
  - File no later than 18 months after tax year end
- **Reporting**: Enter credit amount on line 698 of Schedule 5

#### Business Carbon Price Rebate
- **Components**:
  - General business carbon price rebate (line 699 of Schedule 5)
  - Mining business carbon price rebate (line 696 of Schedule 5)
- **Filing Requirements**: File Schedule 444, Yukon Business Carbon Price Rebate
- **Reference**: Carbon Price Rebate General Regulation

### Northwest Territories Tax Rates and Credits

#### Tax Rates
- **Lower Rate**: 2% (effective January 1, 2021, previously 4%)
- **Higher Rate**: 11.5%
- **Calculation**: Use Schedule 461, Northwest Territories Corporation Tax Calculation (not filed with return)
- **Reporting**: Enter tax amount on line 250 of Schedule 5

#### Political Contribution Tax Credit
- **Eligibility**: Contributions to candidate for Northwest Territories Legislative Assembly
- **Maximum Credit**: $500 annually
- **Calculation**:
  - 100% of first $100 contributed
  - Plus 50% of next $800 contributed
- **Note**: Contributions to political party do not qualify
- **Reporting**: 
  - Enter contributions on line 898 of Schedule 5
  - Enter credit on line 700 of Schedule 5

### Federal Investment Tax Credits

#### Investment Tax Credit Refund
- **Reporting**: Enter amount on line 780 of Schedule 5
- **Includes**: Clean economy ITCs and refundable part of SR&ED ITC

#### Carbon Capture, Utilization, and Storage (CCUS) ITC
- **Eligibility**: Businesses incurring qualified CCUS expenditures after 2021 and before 2041
- **Credit Rates**:
  - 60% after 2021 (30% after 2030) for direct air capture equipment
  - 50% after 2021 (25% after 2030) for other capture equipment
  - 37.5% after 2021 (18.75% after 2030) for transportation, storage, and use equipment
- **Labour Requirements**: 
  - Must elect to meet prevailing wage and apprenticeship requirements (effective November 28, 2023)
  - Failure to meet requirements reduces credit rate by 10 percentage points
- **CCA Classes**:
  - Class 57: 8% rate for capture, transportation, and storage equipment
  - Class 58: 20% rate for use equipment
  - Classes 59 and 60: for exploration and development expenses related to CO2 storage
- **Tracking Requirements**: Corporations must track and account for capture and actual usage of CO2
